About Silksong

Silksong is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed game Hollow Knight. In this new adventure, players will take on the role of Hornet, a skilled warrior and protector of Hallownest.

Discover a vast and beautiful world filled with new enemies, characters, and secrets.

Experience new mechanics and gameplay elements that build upon the foundation of Hollow Knight.
